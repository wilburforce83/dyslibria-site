Features

Fully open source; self-host on a Raspberry Pi, VPS, or another home server setup.

Bulk conversion of epub files into bionic text either via the web portal or drag and drop into the upload directory.

Editable processed folder; send processed files directly into third-party applications such as Calibre for seamless integration.

OPDS out of the box; connect to your favourite e-reader app.

WebDav server built-in for any archaic users out there!

Built-in web portal for uploading books, viewing library, searching for books, and reading online.

What is Bionic Text?

Bionic text refers to a style of text presentation designed to enhance readability for people with visual processing challenges such as dyslexia, ADHD, ADD, and broader neurodivergent audiences. This method improves focus and reading speed by optimizing text appearance without altering the text's integrity, making it more accessible and easier to process.
