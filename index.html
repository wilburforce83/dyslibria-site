What is Bionic Text?

Bionic text refers to a style of text presentation designed to enhance readability for people with visual processing challenges such as dyslexia, ADHD, ADD, and broader neurodivergent audiences. This method improves focus and reading speed by optimizing text appearance without altering the text's integrity, making it more accessible and easier to process.
